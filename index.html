IEEE LaunchPad Kerala

What is Launchpad Kerala ?

One of the most prestigious event of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates.

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

" We invite you to be a part of IEEE LaunchPad Kerala's exciting journey! Join us to connect with like-minded innovators, participate in our dynamic events, and contribute your unique skills and ideas. Together, we can drive innovation and create impactful solutions. Don't miss out get involved today and be a catalyst for change! "

Event Roadmap

Our event journey kicks off with Participant Registration followed by the Company Registration Close. Once the registrations are complete, the Challenge Start sets the stage for innovation and problem-solving. Participants will then undertake an Online Exam to demonstrate their skills. Next, a Consolidated Rank List will be published, showcasing the top performers. Participants will then proceed to Option Selection based on their ranks. Following this, Confirmed Mail to Selected Participants will be sent out. Finally, the event culminates with Interviews for the selected candidates, offering them the opportunity to further demonstrate their abilities.
